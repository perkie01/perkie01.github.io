about me

I am an Associate Degree Candidate in Information Technology and Baccalaureate Degree Candidate in Computer Science at Franklin University. I am deeply committed to community service, actively volunteering with organizations like The Human Rights Campaign and the N.A.A.C.P. Through these organizations I have been afforded the opportunity to volunteer for multiple high profile politicians including Stacey Abrams, Joe Biden, Jon Ossoff, & Raphael Warnock.

I utilized Swift, SwiftUI, and SwiftData. This app serves as a practical tool for learners worldwide. My most recent app (Kamala Now) was created as a means to provide a dedicated location for all things Kamala. Users can watch her livestreams, view various campaign points, donate, or signup to volunteer themselves. Both apps are available on the Apple AppStore.
